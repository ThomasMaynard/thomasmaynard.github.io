Claim

Inside the mystery black box there is a cup filling up with water before dispensing the water at 500ML.

Evidence

Reasoning

This is proven because when the cup is empty and 500 ML of water or less are poured in nothing comes out. When more than 500 ML of water the water comes pouring out which perfectly replicates the first trial of no water.
